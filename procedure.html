Network Topologies

Procedure

To perform the simulation, follow the below given steps:
1) A blank rectangular area is provided which defines the working area.
2) A series of components are given.
3) In order to construct a topology, first select the component and then immediately click on the working area to place it.
4) To draw a cable in between two components, first select the desired cable, then click on the first component and then immediately click on the second component.
5) Once the topology is constructed, click on the evaluation button to verify if the constructed topology is built correctly or not.
6) In order to reset the simulation, click on the reset button.
